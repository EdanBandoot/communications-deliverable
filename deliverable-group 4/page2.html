Academic Success Centre

WHAT WE BELIEVE

The RRC career services offers a number of resources that allow students get access with possible job networks and opputunities.

Supports For Students

It provides a lot of useful information, online and offline support from reading and writing skills to time management and professional communication. If you are a student of RRC, you can participate in the modules for LEARN Course to develop various skills for improving your study efficiency or book an appointment with a tutor to receive tailored service to help you address specific challenges in your studies.

The site offers services such as tutoring appointments, assistance with English language skills, academic writing support, and technology literacy development. It provides students with comprehensive and well-rounded resources and information.

Supports For Faculty and Staff

This platform is designed specifically for staff and employees to access academic support and resources. As an RRC staff member, you can use the website to enhance a variety of skills, including teamwork, communication, time management, and email proficiency.
