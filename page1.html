Career Services

WHAT WE BELIEVE

The RRC career services offers a number of resources that allow students get access with possible job networks and opputunities.

Students and Alumni

Under this section students and recent graduates can book appointments to meet with career advisors and help students build personalized portfolios, LinkedIn porfiles and writing resumes. Students can also get introduced to the work-integrated learning program that can them introduced to their their workplace and real world setting for their specific programs.

Employers

This is designated for employers who are looking to hire graduates and students for their organization. They are able to book events for students and act as guest lecturers to guide students

Staff and Faculty

In this section staff and faculty members can book presentations for students to either help them in workshops or present the enviroment for their work settings, which would allow students to regonize what they are getting into for their job market

Career Bridge

This introduces the students and graduates to the career bridge which is a resource for employers looking for student and graduate to hire for their organizations, allowing students to get possible job oppotunity.

News and Events

In this section of the career services notifies students and graduates of events that are happening at RRC where there is oppunitities to network and look for employer who are looking to hire.
